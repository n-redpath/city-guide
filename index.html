Bend, Orgegon

A paradise for nature and beer lovers

Bend, Oregon is a city in rural oregon. It sits 30 minutes from the desert in one direction, 30 minutes from the mountains in the other

It is surrounded by beauty, making it a hidden gem for outdoorsmen, but the city itself also has a lot to offer!

Bend is home to the most micro breweries in a town under 100,000 people. Beer is a part of the culture, so visiting breweries is a must!

Hiking Information

People come from all over the country and all over the world to hike and mountaineer in the beautiful cascade mountains.

Luckily, there are options for all skill and experience levels. Below is a table of some information on specific hikes

Hike Name Distance Elevation Gain Description
South Sister
 12.2 miles ~5,000ft This is a difficult hike but it is worth it! The views from the glacier at the top are hard to beat, and the trail features beautiful lakes and trees
Blue Pool
 4.1 miles 400 ft This hike is good for all skill levels! It's not too long and the water in the pool at the end is unbelievably blue
Green Lakes
 9.4 miles 2,000 ft This hike is long but not too strenuous, and at the top there is a beautiful lake with views of south sister and broken top!
Broken Top
 8.3 miles 3,000ft This is a must-do. The hike is not to the peak of broken top, but rather to noname lake which comes right before the hike to the summit starts getting technical
Misery Ridge
 3 miles 900ft This is a beautiful, short, but strenuous hike up the ridge at smith rock state park! This park, the birthplace of sport climbing, offers breathtaking views of orange and red colored rocks
Tumalo Mountain Trail
 2.7 miles 800ft this is another short but strenuous hike that offers 360 views of lakes, forests (burnt and healthy) as well as all 3 sisters and broken top!
